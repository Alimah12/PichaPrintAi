about us

our goal is simple: get anyone into hardware prototyping. whether you're a student, artist, entrepreneur, or curious tinkerer, you should be able to bring your ideas to life without friction.

how it works

01

describe your device in plain english. no CAD skills needed.

02

automated generation of circuit diagrams, firmware code, mechanical casing (STL), and a bill of materials.

03

3D print the casing, assemble the circuit, flash the firmware—or let us build it for you.
